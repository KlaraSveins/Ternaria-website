Built To Spec

Tell us your sequence, modification and scale.

Every customized mRNA project starts with your specific requirements — we work with you to define the right construct and production plan, produced with our ArtPure™ technology and specialized transcription kits.

What we customize

Sequence: Your coding sequence and UTRs, defined together with our team.
Modification: Choice of cap analog — including ARCA — and incorporation of modified nucleotides.
Scale: From research-grade quantities to larger production runs.
Shipping: A room-temperature shipping option is available, in addition to standard cold-chain shipment.
Why ArtPure™ mRNA

Built on the same purity standard, start to finish.

Every mRNA product we ship is produced using our own ArtPure™ polymerases, ZebraScribe kits and optimized IVT conditions — so the purity and capping efficiency that define our enzymes carry straight through to your transcript.
