Icelandic Technology Fund grant recipient

Ternaria Biosciences has received R&D grant support from Tækniþróunarsjóður — Iceland's national Technology Development Fund for innovation-driven research. Administered by Rannís (The Icelandic Centre for Research) under the Ministry of Culture, Innovation and Higher Education, the fund backs projects like our AI-driven ArtPure™ enzyme platform from concept through to market.
