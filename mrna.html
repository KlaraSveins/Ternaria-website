eGFP mRNA

Available now

Green Fluorescent Protein (eGFP) mRNA produced with ArtPure™ technology and our specialized transcription kit, for applied research use. GFP is a 238-amino-acid bioluminescent protein from the jellyfish Aequorea victoria, fluorescing at ~510 nm when excited at ~488 nm with no cofactors required — a workhorse reporter across cell biology and physiology.
